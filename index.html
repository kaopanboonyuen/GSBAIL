GSBAIL is the Artificial Intelligence Laboratory of the Government Savings Bank — building trustworthy, scalable, and interpretable AI systems for Thailand's national banking ecosystem.

Meet the Lab → Research Pillars
Mission

We advance AI that a national bank can stand behind — systems that are transparent enough to audit, robust enough to scale across millions of customers, and precise enough to earn the trust every Thai household places in GSB.

Research Focus

Three pillars, one standard.

01 / TRUST

Trustworthy AI

Rigorous evaluation, fairness auditing, and governance frameworks built for regulated financial systems — not bolted on after deployment.

02 / SCALE

Scalable Systems

Infrastructure and model architectures designed to operate reliably at national scale, across millions of accounts and transactions daily.

03 / CLARITY

Interpretable Intelligence

Decisions that can be explained — to regulators, to auditors, and to the customers whose financial lives depend on them.
